Sustainability Through the Ages

Explore Intel's journey through time, discovering how innovation, environmental stewardship, and sustainability initiatives have shaped the future of technology.

1968

Intel Founded

Robert Noyce and Gordon Moore rename NM Electronics to Intel Corporation, laying the foundation for decades of technological innovation.

1971

First Microprocessor

Intel debuts the 4004, the world's first commercial microprocessor.

1978

8086 Processor

Launch of the 8086 processor, establishing the x86 architecture.

1985

386 Processor

Intel introduces the 386 processor with 32-bit architecture.

2006

Peak GHG Emissions

Intel reaches its highest annual greenhouse gas emissions and begins significant sustainability investments.

2020

RISE Strategy

Intel launches its RISE strategy and ambitious 2030 goals.

2022

Net-Zero By 2040

Intel announces its commitment to achieve net-zero greenhouse gas emissions by 2040.

2023

Renewable Electricity

Intel achieves 99% renewable electricity usage worldwide.

2024

Sustainability Summit

Intel hosts its first Sustainability Summit.
